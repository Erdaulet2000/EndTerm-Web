La liga

Example Division (Spanish Primera División) of the Spanish professional football league (Spanish Liga de Fútbol Profesional, LFP), also known as Primera and La Liga (Spanish La Liga), is a top-level professional football tournament in the Spanish football league system. La Liga is considered one of the strongest leagues in the world

Spain cup
The Campeonato de España–Copa de Su Majestad el Rey, commonly known as Copa del Rey or simply La Copa and formerly known as Copa del Generalísimo (1939–76), is an annual knockout football competition in Spanish football, organized by the Royal Spanish Football Federation.
